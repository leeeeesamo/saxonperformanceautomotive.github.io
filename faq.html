FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our mobile mechanic services

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Find answers to common questions about our mobile mechanic services, pricing, and what to expect.

What areas do you service?

We provide mobile mechanic services throughout the local area, including surrounding neighborhoods and rural locations. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just give us a call and we'll confirm right away.

Do you come to my home or workplace?

Yes! We come directly to your home, office, jobsite, or wherever your vehicle is parked. Convenience is the core of our service.

What types of vehicles do you work on?

We service most makes and models — cars, trucks, SUVs, and light-duty vehicles. If you have a specialty vehicle or unique setup, reach out and we can confirm.

What services do you offer?

Our mobile mechanic services include diagnostics, troubleshooting, brake repairs, battery replacement, fluid changes, tune-ups, basic maintenance, and more. Larger repairs may require advance scheduling.

How do I book an appointment?

You can schedule by phone, through our website's contact form, or by clicking the Book Service button. Same-day and next-day appointments are often available.

Do I need to be present during the service?

Not necessarily. As long as the vehicle is accessible and we've confirmed details ahead of time, you don't have to stay onsite for the entire service.

How long does a typical service take?

Basic maintenance usually takes 30–60 minutes. Diagnostics and repairs vary depending on the issue, but we always provide an estimated time before starting.

How do you handle payment?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. All payments are due upon completion of service. Invoices or receipts can be sent by email or text.

Do you offer warranties on parts and labor?

Yes — all parts come with manufacturer warranties, and labor is guaranteed. We stand behind our work and will make it right if something isn't perfect.

What happens if my vehicle needs a major repair?

If the repair can't be safely or realistically performed on-site, we'll walk you through the next steps and recommend trusted local shops we partner with.

Can you diagnose check-engine lights?

Absolutely. We provide full diagnostics using professional-grade tools and explain the results clearly before any repairs are done.

What if it's raining or the weather is bad?

Light rain is usually fine — we can work under portable coverings. Severe weather may require rescheduling for safety, but we'll do our best to keep your appointment.

Are there extra fees for mobile service?

We charge a transparent mobile service/diagnostic fee to come to your location. All pricing is shared upfront — no surprises.

Do you offer emergency or after-hours services?

Depending on availability, we offer rush and after-hours appointments for urgent issues. Call or text to check availability.
