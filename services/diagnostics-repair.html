Diagnostics & Repair

Our diagnostics and repair service focuses on finding the true source of vehicle issues and fixing them correctly the first time.

Diagnostic Process

We use a structured, step-by-step diagnostic approach informed by live data, fault codes, and real-world testing. Instead of guessing and replacing parts, we verify the cause of the issue before recommending repairs.

Common Problems Solved

  • Check engine lights and stored fault codes
  • Engine misfires and rough running
  • Overheating and cooling system issues
  • Electrical faults, shorts, and sensor problems
  • Driveability issues such as hesitation or loss of power

Our Equipment

We use modern scan tools, lab scopes, and testing equipment designed for today’s complex vehicles. This allows us to capture live data, run active tests, and confirm that repairs have resolved the issue.
